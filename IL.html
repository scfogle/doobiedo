Indiana

IN

The state of Indiana still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 30 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 30 g

Results in a Felony,
Up to 3 Years Jail Time

Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony,
2 to 8 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 10 g

Results in Felony, 1 to 3 Years Jail Time>

Cultivation,
Under 5 Plants

Results in Misdemeanor, Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony, 2 to 8 Years Jail Time
