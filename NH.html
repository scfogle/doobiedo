New Hampshire

NH

The state of New Hampshire still views cannabis as a criminal substance. They have passed legislation that acknowledges cannabis as medicinally beneficial but have yet to enact any sort of system to provide citizens with legal access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 1 oz

Results in Felony, Up to 3 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Over 1 lb

Results in Felony, Up to 20 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 5 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 20 Years Jail Time
