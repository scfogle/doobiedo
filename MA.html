Massachusetts

MA

The state of Massachusetts has a decriminalized policy towards personal possession of cannabis and also has recently passed legislation that allows citizens with qualifying medical needs to possess cannabis but as of yet, offers them no means of legal access.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine, No Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Over 1 oz
Second Time

Results in Misdemeanor,
Up to 2 Years Jail Time
