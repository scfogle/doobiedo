Florida

FL

The state of Florida still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 20g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 20g

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Over 25 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 15 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 25 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 25 plants

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 10,000 lb

Results in a Felony,
15 to 30 Years Jail Time
