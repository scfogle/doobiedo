Nebraska

NE

The state of Nebraska still views cannabis as a criminal substance but has a decriminalized policy towards personal possession of small amounts.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine but No Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 3 Months Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Any Amount

Results in a Felony, 1 to 20 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 1 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time
