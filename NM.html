The state of New Mexico has a medical program allowing qualified citizens to legally grow and purchase cannabis for health related needs. Citizens without medical neccesity are still denied access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 15 Days Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 8 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 1.5 Years Jail Time
