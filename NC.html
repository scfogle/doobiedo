NC

The state of North Carolina still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Minor Fine but No Jail Time

Over 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 45 Days Jail Time

Over 1.5 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 8 Months Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 10 lb

Results in Felony,
3 to 8 Months Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 10 lb

Results in Felony,
3 to 8 Months Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony,
