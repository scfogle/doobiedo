TN

The state of Tennessee still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1/2 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 6 Years Jail Time

Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 12 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 1/2 oz

Results in Felony,
Up to 6 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 10 Plants

Results in Felont,
Up to 12 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 300 lb

Results in a Felony,
15 to 60 Years Jail Time
