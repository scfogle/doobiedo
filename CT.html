CONNETICUT

CT

The state of Conneticut has a decriminalized policy on cannabis in small amounts. The state also acknowledges it as medicinally beneficial but has yet to adpot a system which gives its citizens in need any sort of legal access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1/2 oz

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fines No Jail Time


Over 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 4 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 1 kg

Results in Felony, 7 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 1 kg

Results in Felony, 7 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 1 kg

Results in a Felony, 5 to 20 Years Jail Time
