AL

The state of Arizone views cannabis as a criminal substance, prosecuting all who are caught with it and offering no legal means of access for citizens with medical needs.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale, Any Amount

Results in a Felony, 1-10 Years Jail Time

Cultivation, Any Amount

May be treated as either possession or sale.

Trafficking, Over 1 kg

Results in a Felony, 3 years Jail Time
