CALIFORNIA

CA

The state of California has both a decriminalized policy of cannabis regulation as well as a medical program allowing qualified citizens to legally grow and purchase cannabis for health related needs.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 28.5g

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine with No Jail Time

Over 28.5g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Over 1lb

Results in Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale, Any Amount

Results in a Felony, 2 to 4 yrs Jail Time

Cultivation, Any Amount

Results in a Felony, 1 to 3 yrs Jail Time

Trafficking, Any Amount

Results in a Felony, 1 to 3 yrs Jail Time

Medical Regulations:

Personal Possession

Up to 8oz

Personal Cultivation

6 Mature or 12 Immature plants

Sale/Purchase

From Dispenaries, Co-ops and Caregivers
