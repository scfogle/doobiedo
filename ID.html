Idaho

ID

The state of Idaho still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 3 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 3 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 15 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 1 lb

Results in Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time>

Cultivation,
Under 25 Plants

Results in Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 5 lb

Results in a Felony, 3 Years Jail Time
