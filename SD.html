South Dakota

SD

The state of South Dakota still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 2 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1/2 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 1/2 oz

Results in Felony,
Up to 2 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 1 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 15 Years Jail Time
