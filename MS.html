Mississippi

MS

The state of Mississippi has a decriminalized policy towards personal possession of cannabis but still views it as a criminal substance and acknowledges no uses or benefits of the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 30 g

Results in a Citation,
With a Fine but No Jail Time

Over 30 g

Results in a Felony,
Up to 3 years Jail Time

Over 250 g

Results in Felony,
2 to 8 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 30 g

Results in a Felony, Up to 20 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession/Sale of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 5 kg

Results in a Felony, 10 to 30 Years Jail Time
