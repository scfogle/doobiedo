Pennsylvania

PA

The state of Pennsylvania still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 30 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Month Jail Time

Over 30 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 50 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 3 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 2 lb

Results in Felony,
1 Year Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Results in Felony,
1 Year Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 1,000 lb

Results in a Felony,
10 Years Jail Time
