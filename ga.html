Georgia

GA

The state of Georgia still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time


Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 30 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 10 lb

Results in Felony, Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 10 lb

Results in Felony, Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 10,000 lb

Results in a Felony, 15 to 30 Years Jail Time
