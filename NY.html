NY

The state of New York has a decriminalized policy towards personal possession of cannabis but still views it as a criminal substance and acknowledges no uses or benefits of the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 25 g

Results in a Citation,
With a Fine but No Jail Time

Over 25 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 3 Months Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 7 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 25 g

Results in a Felony, Up to 4 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 15 Years Jail Time
