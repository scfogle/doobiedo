Rhode Island

RI

The state of Rhode Island has a medical program allowing qualified citizens to legally grow and purchase cannabis for health related needs. Citizens without medical neccesity are still denied access to the plant but personal possession of small amounts is decriminalized

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Citation,
Fine but No Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 kg

Results in a Felony,
10 to 50 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 1 kg

Results in a Felony, Up to 30 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 1 kg

Results in a Felony, Up to 30 Years Jail Time
