ALASKA

AK

The state of Alaska views cannabis as a criminal substance, yet has legislation in place that allows qualified citizens to grow and possess cannabis for medical needs.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 90 Days Jail Time

Under 4 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 4 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale, Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony, 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation, Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony, 5 Years Jail Time

Trafficking, Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony, 5 years Jail Time

Medical Regulations:

Personal Possession

Up to 1 oz

Personal Cultivation

3 Mature and 3 Immature Plants

Sale/Purchase


IS NOT PERMITTED!
