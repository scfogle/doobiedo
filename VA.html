Virginia

VA

The state of Virginia still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 30 Days Jail Time

Over 1/2 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 5 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 30 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 1/2 oz

Results in Felony,
1 to 10 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Results in Felony,
5 to 30 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 100 kg

Results in a Felony,
20 Years to Life Jail Time
