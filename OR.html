Oregon

OR

The state of Oregon has a medical program allowing qualified citizens to legally grow and purchase cannabis for health related needs. Citizens without medical neccesity are still denied access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Fine but No Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Month Jail Time

Over 4 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Results in a Felony, Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time
