South Carolina

SC

The state of South Carolina still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Month Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 10 lb

Results in a Felony,
1 to 10 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 10 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Over 100 Plants

Results in Felony,
25 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 100 lb

Results in a Felony,
25 Years Jail Time
