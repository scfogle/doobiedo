Delaware

DE

The state of Delaware still views cannabis as a criminal substance. They have passed legislation that acknowledges cannabis as medicinally beneficial but have yet to enact any sort of system to provide citizens with legal access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 5 lb

Results in Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Over 5 lb

Results in Felony, 2 to 25 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 100 lb

Results in a Felony, 4 to 25 Years Jail Time
