Minnesota

MA

The state of Minnesota still views cannabis as a criminal substance and fully prosectute anyone in possession of it, or caught growing or selling the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 42.5 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
With a Fine but No Jail Time

Over 42.5 g

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 years Jail Time

Over 10 kg

Results in Felony,
Up to 20 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 42.5 g

Results in a Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 100 kg

Results in a Felony, Up to 30 Years Jail Time
