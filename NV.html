The state of California has a medical program allowing qualified citizens to legally grow and purchase cannabis for health related needs. Citizens without medical neccesity are still denied access to the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Minor Fine but No Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 100 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time
