KENTUCKY

KY

The state of Kentucky still views cannabis as a fully criminal substance, acknowledging no uses or benefits of the plant. The State will fully prosectue any citizen caught in possession of it.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 8 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 45 Days Jail Time

Over 8 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Over 5 lb

Results in a Felony,
Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 8 oz

Results in Misdemeanor, Up to 1 Year Jail Time>

Cultivation,
Over 5 plants

Results in Felony, 1 to 5 Years Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 5 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 10 Years Jail Time
