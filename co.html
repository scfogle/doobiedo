COLORADO

CO

The state of Colorado views cannabis as a legal substance for adults over 21, However there are still limits set on how much you may lawfully possess, purchase and grow. These limits increase if you use cannabis for medicinal needs.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Is 100% Legal,
No Consequence


Over 1 oz

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine No jail Time

Over 12 oz

Results in a felony,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time


Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Unlicensed sale, Under 5 lb

Results in a Felony, 1 to 3 Years Jail Time

Cultivation


Up to 3 Mature Plants is 100% Legal


Trafficking, Over 100 lb

Results in a Felony, 4 to 12 Years Jail Time

Personal Possession

Up to 2oz

Personal Cultivation

6 Mature plants
