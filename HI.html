The state of Hawaii has legislation in place that allows citizens with medical needs to privately cultivate their own cannabis. The state does not allow them to purchase/sell or distribute cannabis and unless a citizen has a qualifing medical condition, cannabis is still considered a criminal substance.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
up to 30 Days Jail Time

Over 1 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time
