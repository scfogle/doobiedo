The state of Maine has legislation in place that allows citizens with medical needs to Possess, Grow and Purchase Cannabis. It also has a decriminalized policy in place for citizens with out medical neccesity in place.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 2.5 oz

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine, No Jail Time

Over 2.5 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Over 1 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 1 lb

Results in a Misdemeanor, Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Cultivation,
Under 5 Plants

Results in a Misdemeanor, Up to 6 Months Jail Time

Trafficking,
Over 20 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 10 Years Jail Time
