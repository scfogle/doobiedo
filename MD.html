Maryland

MD

The state of Maryland has a decriminalized policy towards personal possession of cannabis and also has recently passed legislation that allows citizens with qualifying medical needs to possess cannabis but as of yet, offers them no means of legal access.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 10 g

Results in a Citation,
Minor Fine, No Jail Time

Over 10 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 3 Months Jail Time

Over 50 lb

Results in Felony,
Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Under 50 lb

Results in a Felony, Up to 5 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

May be treated as either possession or sale

Trafficking,
Over 45 kg

Results in a Felony, Up to 25 Years Jail Time
