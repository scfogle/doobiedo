Missouri

MO

The state of Missouri still views cannabis as a criminal substance and will fully prosecute anyone caught in possession of it. The State acknowledges no uses or benefits of the plant.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 35 g

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 35 g

Results in a Felony,
Up to 1 year Jail Time

Over 30 kg

Results in Felony,
5 to 15 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 5 g

Results in a Felony, 5 to 15 Years Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

Treated as Possession/Sale of Total Weight of Plants Found

Trafficking,
Over 100 kg
