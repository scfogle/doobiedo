ARKANSAS

AR

The state of Arkansas views cannabis as a criminal substance, prosecuting all who are caught with it and offering no legal means of access for citizens with medical needs.

Simple Possession Consequences:

Under 4 oz

Results in a Misdemeanor,
Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Over 4 oz

Results in a Felony,
Up to 6 Years Jail Time

Over 10 lb

Results in a felony,
Up to 10 Years Jail Time

Sale/Cultivation Consequences:

Sale,
Over 14g

Results in a Felony, Up to 1 Year Jail Time

Cultivation,
Any Amount

May be treated as either possession or sale

Trafficking,
Over 500 lb

Results in a Felony, 10 to 40 Years Jail Time
